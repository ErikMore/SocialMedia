You may be wondering what is A3C? Good Question!

A3C (Asynchronous Advantage Actor-Critic) is a reinforcement learning algorithm that is used to train deep neural networks to make decisions in an environment. It is designed to handle large and continuous action spaces, making it useful for a wide range of tasks.Biggest advantage over other models is the ability to achieve Asynchronous updates which allow for faster training.
